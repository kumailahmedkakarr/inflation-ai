Inflation in Pakistan is caused by multiple factors.

1. Increase in money supply.
2. High import dependency.
3. Dollar appreciation.
4. Energy crisis.
5. Supply chain disruptions.
6. Political instability.
7. Increase in petroleum prices.
8. Weak industrial production.

Government solutions include:
- Control monetary policy.
- Support local industries.
- Reduce imports.
- Improve tax system.

Citizen role:
- Support local products.
- Avoid hoarding.
- Save energy.
- Pay taxes honestly.
